Balanced Nutrition & Calorie Basics

Understand macros, portions, and how movement fits into health.

Daily Energy Needs

Your daily calories come from your Basal Metabolic Rate (BMR) times an activity factor. This app uses the Mifflin‑St Jeor equation. For goals, we adjust by ±10–20% thoughtfully.

  • Protein: satiety and muscle repair
  • Carbs: energy for brain and training
  • Fats: hormones and vitamin absorption

Plate Method

At main meals, aim for a plate with roughly half non‑starchy veggies, a quarter lean protein, and a quarter whole grains or starchy veg. Add healthy fats and fruit or dairy as fits your diet.

Movement Matters

Regular activity improves insulin sensitivity, sleep, and mood. Mix cardio with two days of strength training for resilience. Hydrate and prioritize sleep for recovery.

About This Project

Designed to make healthy eating accessible for students, athletes, busy pros, and anyone with dietary needs.

This tool generates practical, allergy‑aware weekly plans. It’s not a substitute for personalized medical advice. For complex conditions, consult a registered dietitian or your clinician.
